Kanshi: Your Personal Anime, Manga, and Light Novel Recommender

What is Kanshi?

Kanshi is a tailored app offeringanime,manga(including manhwa, manhua, and one-shots), andlight novel(LN) recommendations by analyzing yourpersonal ratings on AniList.

Key Features

  • Personalized recommendations based on your AniList ratings
  • Discovers underrated and underseen series aligned with your taste
  • Customizable algorithm with user-chosen features (genres, tags, etc.)
  • Adjustable recommendation thresholds
  • Free to use
  • Runs locally for privacy and convenience

How It Works

Kanshi identifies the genres and themes you love or dislike using your AniList ratings. By eliminating the need to rely on others' preferences, it provides recommendations tailored specifically to your taste.

Available Platforms

  • Website
  • Android Application
